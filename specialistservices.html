Specialist services

Our Specialist Services cater to dogs with unique grooming needs, including Nail Clipping and Hand Stripping. With a Level 3 Dog Grooming Diploma and full insurance, our skilled groomers provide expert care for your pet.
Nail Clipping: This service ensures your dog’s nails are safely trimmed to the correct length. We begin with a gentle health check, then carefully clip the nails to prevent overgrowth or discomfort, ensuring your dog’s paws stay healthy and pain-free.
Hand Stripping: Ideal for breeds with wiry coats, this technique involves manually removing dead hair to maintain coat texture and prevent matting. We use specialized tools and techniques to gently strip the coat, promoting healthy regrowth and giving your dog a polished, natural appearance.
Both services are tailored to meet the specific needs of your dog, providing a professional and stress-free experience.
