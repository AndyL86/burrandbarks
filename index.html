Welcome to Rothwell's newly established premier Dog Grooming Salon, where every pup receives professional, gentle care. Serving Rothwell, Oulton, Woodlesford and nearby Leeds areas, we specialise in grooming all breeds to perfection. Book today for a happy, healthy and beautifully groomed pet!

About Us

Charlotte - Head of Grooming

Hi, my name is Charlotte, I have always dreamed of owning my own Dog Grooming Salon and after 20 years working in the Banking Industry I took the decision to follow my passion and set up Burr and Barks. My goal is to provide not only expert grooming but also a caring, stress-free experience for both pets and their owners. As a proud owner of 2 Cocker Spaniels, I have a deep understanding of every dogs unique needs. ...
Having completed both Level 2 and Level 3 City & Guilds dog grooming courses and being awarded Best Student Level 2 Dog Grooming, I am equipped with the skills and expertise to deliver the highest standards of care for all breeds. I have also worked closely Spaniel Aid dogs charity, supporting their mission and helping improve the lives of these wonderful dogs.

Meet the team...

Hollie - Director of Recreational Activities

Hollie is our 10 year old Working Cocker Spaniel, she is a very loyal, loving and all round fantastic dog. She is great at reading other dogs and is an extremely valued member of the family and the team. Although she is a little too obsessed with tennis balls.

Mabel - Head of Security and Welfare

Mabel is our 6 year old Show Cocker Spaniel, she has been with us over 2 years after intially fostering her from Spaniel Aid dogs charity. She quickly showed us how loving and playful she is and it wasn't a difficult decision to adopt her. Since her arrival she has undergone double cateract surgery and has lost over 3kgs! Doesn't she look great!!
